1. Introduction to Token Investing

What Are Property Tokens?

Property tokens are digital assets that represent fractional ownership of real estate properties. Instead of buying an entire property (which requires hundreds of thousands of dollars), you can:

  • Buy as little as 1 token (often $50-$500 per token)
  • Own a fraction of multiple properties
  • Earn passive income from rental payments
  • Sell your tokens anytime on the B2B marketplace
  • Build a diversified real estate portfolio with less capital

Why Invest in Property Tokens?

Benefit Description
Low Entry Barrier Start investing with as little as $100
Diversification Spread investment across multiple properties and locations
Passive Income Receive your share of rental income automatically
Liquidity Sell tokens on B2B marketplace (unlike traditional real estate)
No Management Landlord/property manager handles everything
Transparency Blockchain-based ownership records

5. Earning Passive Income

How Income Distribution Works:

  1. Tenants Pay Rent: Monthly rent is collected by landlord/property manager
  2. Income Calculation: Platform calculates token holder share (e.g., 40% of rent)
  3. Distribution: Your portion is calculated based on your % of tokens owned
  4. Payment: Funds are deposited to your AssetXToken wallet
  5. Withdrawal: Transfer to your bank account anytime

Income Distribution Schedule:

  • Monthly: Most common - distributions around 5th of each month
  • Quarterly: Some properties distribute every 3 months
  • Annually: Rare, but some luxury properties distribute yearly

6. The B2B Token Marketplace

What is the B2B (Business-to-Business) Market?

The B2B Token Marketplace is a secondary market where token holders can buy and sell tokens from each other. This provides liquidity that traditional real estate investments lack.

Selling Your Tokens:

  1. Go to your Portfolio Dashboard
  2. Select tokens you want to sell
  3. Click "List for Sale"
  4. Set your asking price:
    • At Market Rate: Original purchase price
    • Premium: Higher if property value increased
    • Discount: Lower for quick sale
  5. Choose listing duration (30, 60, 90 days)
  6. Submit listing to B2B marketplace
  7. Other buyers can purchase your tokens
  8. Receive payment minus platform fee (typically 2-3%)

Buying from B2B Marketplace:

  • Browse listings from other investors
  • Often find tokens at discounts (sellers who need quick cash)
  • Instant ownership transfer (no landlord approval needed)
  • Same income rights as primary token holders
üí° Investment Strategy: Some investors focus on "flipping" undervalued tokens on the B2B market for quick profits, similar to stock trading.

8. Investment Strategies

Strategy 1: Passive Income Focus

Goal: Maximize monthly cash flow

Approach:

  • Target properties with high rental yield (8-12% annual ROI)
  • Focus on stable, fully-occupied properties
  • Prefer monthly distributions over quarterly
  • Reinvest dividends to compound returns

Best For: Investors seeking supplemental income

Strategy 2: Capital Appreciation Focus

Goal: Maximize token value growth

Approach:

  • Invest in emerging neighborhoods with growth potential
  • Accept lower initial yields for higher appreciation
  • Hold long-term (3-5+ years)
  • Sell tokens on B2B market when property values increase

Best For: Long-term wealth building

Strategy 3: B2B Trading

Goal: Profit from market inefficiencies

Approach:

  • Buy undervalued tokens on B2B marketplace
  • Monitor market trends and property news
  • Sell when tokens reach fair value or premium
  • Shorter holding periods (weeks to months)

Best For: Active investors with market knowledge

Strategy 4: Balanced Portfolio

Goal: Mix of income and growth

Approach:

  • 60% in stable, high-yield properties
  • 30% in growth/appreciation properties
  • 10% for B2B trading opportunities
  • Regular rebalancing every 6 months

Best For: Most investors
