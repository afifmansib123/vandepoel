# Legal Documents

This directory contains the legal documents for AssetXToken:

- `terms.pdf` - Terms of Service
- `privacy.pdf` - Privacy Policy

## Uploading Documents

To upload or update these documents:

1. Log in as a Super Admin
2. Navigate to `/superadmins/documents`
3. Upload the PDF files for Terms of Service and Privacy Policy

The documents will be automatically available for download in the website footer.

## Note

If the PDF files don't exist, users will get a 404 error. Make sure to upload the documents from the superadmin dashboard before going live.
